Petunia

Hi, my name is Petunia!
I live in the city of Tree Fields,
and I live in the most beautiful field!

Since I was a young creature, I have faced struggles with my short arms,
but that hasn't stopped me from living the life of my dreams!

I use my legs to grab objects, and sometimes even my spiky ears!
They are great at grabbing apples off trees.

Some of my hobbies include
reading, eating food, dancing to music, and lounging outside.

The weather in Tree Fields is always sunny and warm,
which is my favorite.
I love working on my tan!

If I were to describe myself in 3 words,
I would say I am optimistic, friendly, and kind.

I love to meet new people,
and can't wait to have more adventures
and fun times here in Tree Fields!

Hope to see you again soon!
